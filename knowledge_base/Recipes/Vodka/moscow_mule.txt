Raise the Bar Standard Moscow Mule

Base Spirit: Vodka

Build Spec:
- 2 oz Vodka
- 0.5 oz Fresh Lime Juice
- 4 oz Ginger Beer

Method:
Build in a highball glass over ice. Stir gently to integrate without excessive carbonation loss.

Glassware:
Copper Mug or Collins Glass

Garnish:
Lime Wedge
