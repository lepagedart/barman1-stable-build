Raise the Bar House Spritz Template

Tags: Spritz, Highball, Aperitivo, Batchable, House Build, Non-Alcoholic Variant Available

---

COCKTAIL NAME: House Spritz  
BASE SPIRIT: Aperitivo or Amaro (e.g. Aperol, Cappelletti, Cynar)

---

BUILD SPEC:
- 2 oz Aperitivo or Amaro  
- 2 oz Brut Prosecco  
- 1–1.5 oz Soda Water (to taste)

---

METHOD:
1. Build directly in a wine glass or large rocks glass over ice  
2. Gently stir to integrate — do not over-agitate carbonation  
3. Adjust soda ratio for dilution preference or volume pacing

---

GARNISH:
- Large citrus slice (orange, grapefruit, or lemon depending on base)
- Optional: olive or rosemary sprig for house flair

---

NOTES:
This is Raise the Bar’s default spritz build for both traditional and house-modified aperitivo programs.  
Ideal for batching the aperitivo base. Prosecco and soda should always be added to order to preserve effervescence.  
Non-alcoholic variants can be built using NA aperitivos (e.g. Lyre’s, Ghia) using the same structure.
