Smokey Java negroni
Tags: mezcal, stirred, negroni, coffee liqueur, after-dinner, bitter-sweet, fall-winter

Build:
	•	1 oz Mezcal
	•	1 oz sweetened espresso (5:1 fresh brewed espresso:granulated sugar by volume)
	•	1 oz Aperol

Method:
Shake with ice and strain over a large ice cube in a rocks glass. Garnish with a coffee bean and orange twist.

Notes:
The coffee liqueur adds depth and richness, making this a strong candidate for a dessert or after-dinner cocktail.
