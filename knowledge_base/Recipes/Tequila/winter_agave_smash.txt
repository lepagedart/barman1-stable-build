Winter Agave Smash
Tags: añejo tequila, shaken, winter citrus, fresh herbs, seasonal, aromatic, mid-volume service

Build:
	•	2 oz Añejo Tequila
	•	0.75 oz Velvet Falernum
	•	0.5 oz Lemon Juice
	•	Fresh Mint Leaves

Method:
Muddle mint gently with lemon juice. Add remaining ingredients and shake with ice. Double strain over fresh ice into a rocks glass. Garnish with a mint sprig.

Notes:
A rich and herbaceous smash build that keeps the warmth of añejo tequila while incorporating bright seasonal flavors.
