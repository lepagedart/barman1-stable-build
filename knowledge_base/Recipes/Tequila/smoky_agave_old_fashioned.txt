Smoky Agave Old Fashioned
Tags: mezcal, stirred, winter, old fashioned variation, chocolate bitters, low-volume, strong builds

Build:
	•	2 oz Mezcal
	•	0.25 oz Agave Nectar
	•	2 dashes Chocolate Bitters
	•	2 dashes Orange Bitters

Method:
Stir with ice and strain over a large clear cube in a rocks glass. Garnish with an orange peel and single star anise.

Notes:
A sophisticated take on an Old Fashioned using smoky mezcal, adding depth and warmth with subtle chocolate and citrus notes.
