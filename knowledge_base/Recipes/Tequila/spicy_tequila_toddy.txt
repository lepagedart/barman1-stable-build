Spicy Tequila Toddy
Tags: tequila, hot cocktail, toddy, winter, spiced, honey syrup, chili bitters, citrus, high-volume prep

Build:
	•	2 oz Reposado Tequila
	•	0.75 oz Honey Syrup (1:1 honey to hot water)
	•	0.5 oz Lemon Juice
	•	2 dashes of Chili Bitters
	•	Hot Water to top

Method:
Build directly in a tempered mug. Stir gently. Garnish with a lemon wheel and cinnamon stick.

Notes:
Offers a warming, spiced profile perfect for winter, with a nod to traditional hot toddies.
