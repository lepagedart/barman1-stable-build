Pom-pom Sour (Tequila Sour with a Twist)
Tags: tequila, shaken, egg white, sour, pomegranate, seasonal, visual garnish, mid-volume build

Build:
	•	2 oz Blanco Tequila
	•	0.75 oz Lime Juice
	•	0.5 oz Simple Syrup
	•	0.5 oz Pomegranate Juice
	•	0.5 oz Egg White

Method:
Dry shake, then shake with ice and fine strain into a coupe. Garnish with pomegranate seeds and a lime wheel.

Notes:
This sour features a wintry twist with pomegranate, bringing color, freshness, and visual impact.
