Cocktail: The Aurora Borealis

**Build Method:** Shaken and layered  
**Glassware:** Highball glass or Collins glass  
**Garnish:** Edible flower and a dusting of edible glitter

#### Ingredients:
- 1 oz Gin
- 0.75 oz Elderflower liqueur
- 0.75 oz Lemon juice
- 0.5 oz Blue Curacao
- 0.5 oz Simple syrup
- 1 oz Soda water (to top)
- 1 Egg white (optional for texture and foam)

#### Instructions:
1. **Shake**: Combine gin, elderflower liqueur, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white in a shaker with ice. Shake vigorously for a creamy texture.
2. **Strain**: Fine strain into a highball glass half-filled with fresh ice.
3. **Layer**: Carefully pour the Blue Curacao over the back of a spoon to create a layered effect.
4. **Top**: Add soda water gently to maintain the layers.
5. **Garnish**: Finish with an edible flower and a light dusting of edible glitter for visual impact.yeah
