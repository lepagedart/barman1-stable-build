## Updated Plan (Post-Rubric)

### Pricing Guardrails
- Price band: **$13–$15**; aim **≤22% pour cost** (≤24% if elderflower/cream).
- Track: item COGS, menu-mix %, voids/comps.

### Per-Drink Adjustments
1. **Citrus Sky (Vodka/Elderflower/Lemon/Soda)** – *$14*  
   - Spec tweak: reduce elderflower to **0.25–0.5 oz**; add **expressed lemon oil** instead of extra liqueur.  
   - Batch: vodka + elderflower + simple (**1L** @ 8–10% ABV), add lemon & soda a la minute.  
   - Cost: consider **house elderflower syrup** (floral tea + sugar + citric) to cap liqueur usage.

2. **Sunset Paloma (Tequila/Grapefruit Soda/Lime/Agave/Salt)** – *$13*  
   - Swap: use **grapefruit cordial (peel-infused) + soda** (2:1 cordial:soda) to control cost/consistency.  
   - Prep: pre-batch **tequila + agave**; half-rim salt only.  
   - NA twin: **Grapefruit Cooler** (cordial + lime + soda).

3. **Smoky Old Fashioned (Bourbon/Smoked Syrup/Bitters)** – *$15*  
   - Syrup: standardize smoked syrup at **1:1** (smoke the water, not the sugar) for stability; **0.25–0.35 oz** per drink.  
   - Option: **0.25 oz mezcal split** upsell; keep baseline bourbon for cost control.  
   - Batch: keep bitters separate; pre-batch whiskey + syrup if ticket times climb.

4. **Garden Gimlet (Gin/Cucumber/Lime/Basil)** – *$14*  
   - Speed: replace muddle with **cucumber-basil cordial** (cucumber water + basil steep + sugar + acid).  
   - Garnish: single **basil leaf**; skip cucumber wheel.  
   - NA twin: **Cucumber Basil Cooler** (cordial + lime + soda).

5. **Island Drift (Rum/Pineapple/Coconut Cream/Lime)** – *$14*  
   - Balance: reduce coconut cream to **0.5 oz**; **whip-shake** for speed/texture.  
   - Low-ABV variant: **Coconut-Pine Swizzle** with **fino sherry + coconut water**.

### Layout & Flow (Menu)
- Order: **Citrus Sky → Sunset Paloma → Garden Gimlet → Smoky Old Fashioned → Island Drift**.  
- Micro-descriptions (7–10 words), one icon max per item; highlight **Staff Pick** (Gimlet) and **New** (Negroni-style add if you add it).  
- Optional sixth slot: **“Bitter Bright” (Gin, amaro, orange oil)** stirred, $14.

### Prep & Station
- **Batches (1L):** Vodka-Elderflower-Simple; Tequila-Agave; Cucumber-Basil Cordial; Smoked Syrup.  
- **Shelf life:** cordials/syrups **4–7 days** (label & date).  
- **Ticket time target:** ≤ **3:30**; measure Fri peak every 30 min.

### KPIs (first 2 weeks)
- Cocktail COGS **≤ 22%**; Avg sell price **≥ $13.75**.  
- Top-2 items **≥ 38%** of mix.  
- Ticket time **≤ 3:30** avg; NA/Low-ABV attach **≥ 6%**.
