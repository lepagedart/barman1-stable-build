For new or basic cocktail menus, focus on:
- Core spirit families (whiskey, gin, tequila, rum, vodka)
- Familiar formats (sours, highballs, daiquiris, old fashioneds, spritzes, martinis, and any riffs on these formats)
- Balance across spirit categories and flavor profiles
- Simplicity, clarity, and staff-friendly execution
- Inclusion of at least one non-alcoholic option

Avoid excessive flair or complexity. The goal is confidence and consistency across a foundational menu.
