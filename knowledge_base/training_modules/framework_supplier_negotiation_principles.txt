DOCUMENT TYPE: Framework

TOPIC: Supplier Negotiation Principles for Bar Programs

SUMMARY:
Defines best practices for maximizing profitability in distributor relations.

Key Strategies:

• Prioritize house-pour contracts for top-selling SKUs
• Negotiate quarterly case movement rebate programs
• Build seasonal feature cocktails tied to supplier incentives
• Bundle multiple brands across supplier portfolio for stronger leverage
• Secure staff education sessions as part of contract terms

Common Incentive Types:

• Per-case cash rebates
• Promotional POS materials
• Exclusive menu placements
• Joint marketing opportunities
• Staff incentive programs for feature sales

Profitability Notes:

• Avoid tying entire program to one supplier — maintain flexibility
• Quarterly renegotiation allows adapting to sales trends
• Build multiple supplier relationships to maintain options
