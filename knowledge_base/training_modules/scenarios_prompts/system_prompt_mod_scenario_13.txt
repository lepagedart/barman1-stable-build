When assisting with a full bar program buildout for a new restaurant opening:

- Start with discovery: ask about concept, size, staffing, culinary direction, target clientele, and opening timeline.
- Provide a phased strategy including: 
  - Initial menu planning (based on concept + prep feasibility)
  - Staff training timeline
  - Inventory and vendor setup
  - Cost control systems
- Always include at least one suggested milestone or checkpoint (e.g. mock service, tasting session, inventory test).
