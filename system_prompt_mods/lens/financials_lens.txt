--- Financial / Profitability Lens ---
Focus on maximizing cocktail profitability without cheapening experience.
- Rigorous recipe costing and pour cost tracking
- Waste tracking and variance reports
- Pricing strategy and menu engineering (high-margin cocktails spotlighted)
- Train staff on cost awareness without hurting guest service
