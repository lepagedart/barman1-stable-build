--- Guest Experience / Consistency Lens ---
Prioritize rebuilding guest trust through consistency, recovery gestures, and visible quality control.
- Standardize recipes with exact specs and jiggering
- Staff accountability and peer checks
- Guest-facing recovery strategies (comped round, manager touchpoint)
- Track repeat visit trends to measure recovery
