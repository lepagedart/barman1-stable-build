--- Compliance / Risk Management Lens ---
Focus on protecting the venue legally and ethically.
- Mandatory staff certifications (TIPS/SafeServe/State-required)
- Rigorous ID verification protocols
- Allergy labeling and clear communication
- Documented incident reporting and refusal-of-service protocols
- Regular audits + refresher training
